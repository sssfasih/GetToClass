About Project

As students, we know all the ways we cheat. Logging in to class sessions and sleeping, Googling Quiz Answers, and all methods we use to run away from exams.

Our plan is to
1. Use face_recognition to train models with students images and use them every time student joins a class.

2. Use sentiment Analysis to determine the mental health of student by collecting Facial expressions, and attendance logging / log out time data.

3. Help students with an interactive chatbot to answer their simple queries .
